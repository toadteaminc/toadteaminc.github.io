REMOVE, PROTECT, PRESERVE

Toad Team's mission is to effectively and humanely remove invasive Cane Toads from our local florida ecosystems. This protects our beloved cats and dogs, as well as restoring the native species that once thrived here.

We are a Non-Profit Organization that serves the community. We will stop by and remove as many toads as we can from your area. Just give us a call or text at 239-307-8259 and we will be happy to help.

Toad Team's Goals

Remove

To remove the invasive Cane Toads (also known as bufo toads) we use humane methods to capture and euthanize.

Protect

Cane Toads hold enough toxins to kill a dog within 15 minutes after contact. Our job is to remove as many as possible to protect our furry friends.

Preserve

When removing the invasive species we give way for the native species to thrive once again in our beautiful Florida Ecosystems.
